# Privacy Policy

My Sushi respects your privacy.

This app is designed as a personal log for recording sushi restaurants you have visited. We do not collect, store, or share personal information on external servers.

## Information Collection and Use
- Any data you create in the app (such as notes, photos, or locations) is stored locally on your device.
- The app does not require user accounts or sign-in.
- We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personally identifiable information.

## Photos
- Photos are accessed only when you choose to attach them to your records.
- Photo data is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to any server.

## Location Information
- Location access is used only to save visited places on the map.
- Location data is not tracked in the background and is not shared with third parties.

## Third-Party Services
- My Sushi does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties.

## Changes to This Policy
- This privacy policy may be updated in the future.
- Any changes will be reflected in this document.
